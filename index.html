By Elara Vance 342 Pages Fantasy / Mystery

Synopsis

In a world where stars are whispering secrets to those who listen, young astronomer Leo discovers an ancient manuscript hidden within the walls of the Great Observatory. As the constellations begin to shift in ways that defy physics, Leo must lead his family through a series of celestial trials to prevent the eternal eclipse. A story of heritage, hope, and the light that binds us together.
